Membership Terms and Conditions

By becoming a member of Diaspora Credit Union (DCU), you agree to the following terms:

1. Membership Requirements
  • Must be a Zimbabwean national or have Zimbabwean heritage
  • Complete membership application form
  • Provide required identification documents
  • Pay applicable registration fee
2. Shares and Deposits
  • Minimum share purchase required based on membership category
  • Regular monthly deposits mandatory
  • Shares are non-withdrawable but transferable
3. Loan Policies
  • Minimum 3 months membership for loan eligibility
  • Loan amounts based on deposit history
  • Guarantors may be required
4. Data Protection

We comply with data protection regulations and will not share your information without consent.

5. Amendments

Terms may be amended with 30 days notice to members.
